## Consent Banner & Modal

* Consent banner — GDPR-compliant cookie banner with accept/reject/customize actions
* Consent modal — detailed category selection (e.g. analytics, marketing, functional)
* Persistent consent — stores consent decisions in a cookie

## Content Gating

* Content gating — hides third-party content (maps, videos) until the user consents to the relevant category
* Standort Karte — Leaflet.js map with OpenStreetMap tiles; loads only after user consents to the mapping category

## Backend Statistics

* Backend statistics — consent acceptance rates per category in a backend module
